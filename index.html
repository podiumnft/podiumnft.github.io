About

SweetCone NFT is a collection of 5555 colorful cones which consist of more than hundreds of different traits and hand-picked traits.

The buyer and seller of the highest price sold NFT during the week will split royalties in the ratio of 71 and 29 percent. If there is more than one transaction with the same highest price, only the first will be counted. Thus, the seller will list the NFT at a higher price, and the buyers will buy at a higher price, leading to an increase in the floor price. In this sense, the holders will benefit from it.

Owning a SweetCone will also give you access to the community of other sweetcone owners where you can interact with each other. And give you early bird access to the upcoming projects.
